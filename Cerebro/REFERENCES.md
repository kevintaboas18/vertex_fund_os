# References and Methodological Basis

## User-provided Ruta 2030 documents

- *The Wall Street Agent Rulebook* - category weights, 0-10 scoring philosophy, category rules, final verdicts, and overrides.
- *Analisis de Negocio* - ROIC, NOPAT, invested capital, ROIC-WACC spread, margins, FCF conversion, sustainable growth, moat, and business-agent output logic.
- *Agente de Analisis Financiero - Framework de Scoring Aprobado* - 27 core financial metrics, fixed bands, points, verdict, and overrides.
- *Market Opportunity Scorecard* - six-category point architecture, technical profile, opportunity gates, and repeatable workflow.

## Public methodological references

- Aswath Damodaran, NYU Stern: public valuation spreadsheets and papers covering DCF, WACC, bottom-up beta, synthetic ratings, APV, R&D capitalization, leases, economic profit, FCFE/FCFF, and model selection.
- U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission: EDGAR submissions and XBRL company-facts APIs for filing-derived data.
- Andrew W. Lo, Harry Mamaysky, and Jiang Wang: systematic pattern-recognition framework for reducing subjectivity in technical analysis.
- Standard corporate-finance identities and market-risk measures as explicitly defined in this repository.

## Important limitation

The formulas are publicly documented, institutional-grade techniques. They are not claimed to be proprietary intellectual property, secret models, or the exact internal process of any named investment bank, hedge fund, or asset manager.
